# Inequalities

## AM-GM inequality

The AM-GM (arithmetic mean/geometric mean) inequality states that if $x_1, x_2, \dots, x_n$ are nonnegative real numbers, then
\\[\frac{x_1 + x_2 + \dots + x_n}{n} \geq \sqrt[n]{x_1 \cdot x_2 \cdots x_n}.\\]

## Cauchy-Schwarz inequality
(See [AoPS](https://artofproblemsolving.com/wiki/index.php/Cauchy-Schwarz_Inequality))
For any real numbers $a_1, a_2, \dots, a_n$ and $b_1, b_2, \dots, b_n,$
<p>
\[\begin{align}&(a_1^2 + a_2^2 + \dots + a_n^2)(b_1^2 + b_2^2 + \dots + b_n^2) \\
&\qquad\geq (a_1b_1 + a_2b_2 + \dots + a_nb_n)^2.\end{align}\]
</p>
